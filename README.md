# DatSimonBot v1.3.0
A telegram bot that makes  student life easier

## Changelog:
- Fixed some errors
- Added new morning functionality
- Will now show weather
- Set up schedule for further use later

## Bot functions and features:
### University plan management
- The bot is able to save your plan and display it. Additionally you can make changes to these plans with simple button mechanics.
- You can add lessons appearing every week and once in 2 weeks.
- You can check which groups of people are free right now and who is on lesson.
### Group activities (beta)
- the bot can store group-wide activities and display them in neat paging
### Gif management
- DSB can store gifs under a tag which allows to reply to someone with a gif without searching through your entire collection of gifs.
- Multiple gifs can be stored under the same tag and the one sent is random among them.
### Stickers management
- Same as the gifs but with stickers
### Group anti fellony
- The bot will prevent someone from reposting the same thing as someone before. (beta)
### Contexto game
- Allows you to play contexto with friends on a groupchat
### Weather
- Has ability to check weather in given cities
### More features coming soon
